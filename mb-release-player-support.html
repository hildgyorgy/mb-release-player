ShoppingCards

Shopping lists, organized as cards

About

ShoppingCards is a simple, visual way to create and manage shopping lists. Lists are organized as cards, making it quick to switch between them, check off items and keep frequently used entries close at hand.

The app is designed specifically for iPhone, iPad and Mac, with an interface adapted to the way each device is used.

How to use

  • Create a card for each shop, category or shopping trip.
  • Add items by typing or selecting suggestions from your personal library.
  • Tap an item to mark it as completed.
  • Reorder cards and items whenever needed.
  • Use Private Pack for your own lists and Shared Pack for lists you manage with others.

Sync and sharing

ShoppingCards uses Apple iCloud and CloudKit to keep your data synchronized between your devices.

Shared Packs use Apple's built-in CloudKit sharing system. Only people you explicitly invite can access a shared pack, and you can manage participants through Apple's sharing interface.

Privacy Policy

ShoppingCards does not require a separate account and does not operate its own external data server.

Your cards, items, personal item library and app settings are stored locally on your device and, when iCloud is enabled, in your personal Apple iCloud account using CloudKit.

Private Pack data remains private to your iCloud account. Shared Pack data is made available only to participants you explicitly invite through Apple's CloudKit sharing system.

ShoppingCards does not sell personal information and does not use your shopping data for advertising.

Apple may process iCloud and CloudKit data as part of providing its services, subject to Apple's own privacy terms and policies.

If you disable iCloud for ShoppingCards, synchronization and shared packs may no longer be available, while locally stored data may remain on your device.
